Members of the Month

Key Club members that have displayed exceptional service over the course of the month may be named the Member of the Month.
Members of the Month are given rewards for going above and beyond in being active in Key Club.
As laid out in the 2017 SPHS Key Club Specifications, Member of the Month is awarded to a member given that:
  • they have accrued the most points that month.
  • they have not been awarded it the previous month.
  • they are not a board member.
Ties are broken by decision of the Key Club Board.
