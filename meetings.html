General & Board Meetings

At the start of each month, Key Club comes together to discuss events and hour opportunities among the board and committee members, as well as with all other Key members.
​
Board Meetings
Board meetings take place at least monthly and are closed to all members. Board meetings are somewhat spontaneous and occur whenever an issue needs in-depth discussion, as we continuously communicate through alternative means. The best way to stay informed is to stay tuned through Remind and Facebook.

General Meetings
Every month, we hold a general meeting for all members, which takes place in either the band room or the Little Theatre. It is important that all members attend general meetings, as future and current point opportunities and social events are presented at them. However, in the event that you are unable to attend a general meeting, don't worry! You can find the agenda here or find current opportunities on the Facebook page.
 

Zone Meetings

The Key Club Kickoff Conference (KCKC) and Spring Zone Rally (SZR) are our zone meetings. KCKC is typically in the fall, and SZR is typically in the spring. At these meetings, members gather with different schools from across our zone.  At these meetings, members go to different trainings, and they can meet with members from their division as well. There are typically meetings that overview different organizations that Key can volunteer with as well. They are open to any members in Key Club.

District Education and Leadership Conference

The Florida District Education and Leadership Conference (DCON) encompasses all of Florida's Key Clubs and their members. It is attended by over 2,000 students and chaperones from both Florida and the Cayman Islands annually. It comprises a few days of attending workshops and caucuses, listening to motivational speakers, attending numerous social events, and meeting other Key Clubbers. At DCON, new district officers are elected, candidates for international trustee are endorsed, and individual and club awards are presented. There are scholarships available to win. There is also a talent show for each annual convention.

SPHS Key Club has a few requirements each year that members must meet to qualify to attend DCON in the spring. For more information about DCON, stay tuned on the SPHS Key Club Facebook page. Additionally, you can find FAQs at the Florida Key Club website.
 

Key Club International Convention

The Key Club International Convention (ICON) is held in the summer and is open to members of Key Club from all over the world. ICON presents an amazing opportunity to grow your leadership skills and learn how to make a larger impact on your school and community. You can also register to attend ICON virtually, with livestreamed workshops and main events. For more information, you can contact a board member or see the registration page.
